[UPDATE] On April 5th 2005, Frank Joergenson predicted Milwaukee VS Baltimore would result in a shocking 27-0 win for the Orioles. This huge upset and the reverse spread was a x80 bet if the Orioles won by 19 points or more. Frank Jeorgenson sold his 1983 Pinto and his Laptop for $500, in addition to withdrawing his savings that consisted of his life-long winnings of $300. He bet in total $805.73 on the game. The game resulted in a 7-3 victory for the Brewers. After losing all of his money on what he reffered to as the "prophesied game", Frank Joergenson was found dead by heart failure in his bachelors apartment 3 months after his death. In memory of Frank and his contributions to this science, I, dourgh beach, will continue to host and maintain this FAQ as necessary. We'll miss you Frank, may you chart forever in the afterlife.
