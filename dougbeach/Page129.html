1st Base - Prepare. Any charter worth his salt has at least 10 years worth of baseball stats cycled yearly. Don't buy *CHEAP* charts either if you think it's going to save you money, because it won't!

2nd Base - Predict. Use the math algorithms and quadruple-check the results. Sometimes you might be spending a few hours doing this, but you need to keep at it to get faster. I can chart a game in under 45 minutes, even though I spent 7 hours the first day.

3rd Base - Choose. Use the Decoder Strip to get the percentile curve for the games. This will tell you which games are *HOT* and which games aren't. Only bet 1 or two games your first weeks of charting.

Home Base - Wager. To make the over 50% average work in your favour, you need to bet accordingly. On your first ticket bet $5. If you lose, bet $10. If you lose again bet $20. And keep doubling until you win. As you see this ensures that *EVENTUALLY* when you win, you make money even though you may have lost!
Super Tip: Power chart and make double the money! Instead of betting one ticket a day, bet two or three using the same increment amount. Super boost your potential and bet every game every day! Now that's what I call *SUPER* charging your power chart!
