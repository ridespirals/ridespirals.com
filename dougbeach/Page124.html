**** [SET99] WHAT DO I NEED TO START CHARTING ****

Before you can even think about charting, there's a few things you need:

Step 1: Back-up and delete your hard disk. For BEOS users you can do this by dragging your SYSTEM folder into the garbage pail and pressing the delete button 5 times. Put all your vital data on floppy diskettes before deleting.

Step 2: Partition your hard disk data drive. Allocate 200kb of disk space to one drive, and the remaining space to your main drive. Reinstall your standard OS and back-up data on the larger partition.

Step 3: Download and install the Score Charting Planner OS created by Karl Hoppelheimer on the 200kb partition. It's free for the "simple" version but it's not good enough for true charting. Pick up the pro version for $49.99.
Super Tip: You can put the OS on a bootable floppy diskette too! Just make a .bat file and merge it onto the .tiz file for the OS and enter this code at the prompt: conglomerate -x myfile.bat + scoreos.tiz /r
